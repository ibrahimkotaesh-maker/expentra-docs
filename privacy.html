Welcome to Expentra! This Privacy Policy describes how your personal and financial information is handled safely and securely when you use our application.

1 Minimal Information Collection

We aim to collect only the absolute minimum necessary information required for the app to function properly. The majority of your financial data and configurations are processed safely, ensuring a smooth and personalized experience. We profoundly value your privacy. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise exploit your personal or financial data to any third parties for marketing or analytical purposes. Your data is used exclusively to provide, maintain, and improve the services directly within the Expentra ecosystem.

3 Data Security

We utilize industry-standard, robust backend infrastructure (including secure authentication via Supabase) to keep your credentials and configurations safeguarded against unauthorized access.

Questions or Concerns?
